About Us

Welcome to AGED

AGED is a team of indivisuals who are driven towards educating old people who somehow couldn't study at their time.

We provide various courses for people who want to learn something new, be it technology or mathematics or anything else. It all started with our founder feeling bad for her grandmother who was unable to operate her new phone. This gave her the idea to start a new venture to educate our elders.
